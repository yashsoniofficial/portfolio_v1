01 — About

Bringing robots into the real world.

I'm a robotics & automation engineer from Pune, India, now based in Rochester, NY. My mechanical-engineering foundation grew through competitions, drone design and automated test rigs into industrial automation — integrating robots, PLCs and vision into systems that run on real production floors.

Today I'm an Automation Design Engineering Associate at VeroTouch, redeveloping an on-site 3D concrete-printing system, while finishing my M.S. in Mechanical Engineering (Robotics) at RIT. Earlier I spent two-plus years as a software engineer at Searce, building automation pipelines and scalable cloud solutions.

▮ Graduate research @ RIT

Real-time adaptive control systems for bio-inspired actuators — feedback-driven algorithms in LabVIEW and MATLAB/Simulink achieving 10 ms response times, with interdisciplinary work on predictive modeling and embedded-control reliability.
