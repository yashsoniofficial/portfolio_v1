A competition-driven robotics project involving the design and development of two autonomous robots for participation in ABU Robocon 2021, themed around the traditional East Asian game "pitch-pot", As Mechanical Department Head, the individual led the complete design and manufacturing process, including CAD modeling, mechanism development, and integration with electronic systems and microcontrollers, The robots incorporated lightweight aluminum and 3D-printed components, actuated by pneumatic cylinders, and were controlled via Arduino boards with wireless communication using PS5 controllers, The software, developed in C++ using the Arduino IDE, enabled real-time strategy execution during matches.
